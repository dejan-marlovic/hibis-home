Hibis Academy Course Terms

Admission

Course participants register electronically in writing on Hibis Academy's homepage (http://www.Hibisgroup.com/academy). Admissions are accepted in the received order, and are legally binding.

Venue and Cancellation by Hibis Academy

The training venue will be confirmed 14 working days before the course. In the unusual event that there are not sufficient participants, Hibis Academy reserves the right to cancel the course, providing at least 14 working- days of notice to those registered as well as a full refund of any payment which has already been made.

Cancellation by Participant

Any changes in the course registration must be sent to: course@Hibisgroup.com, or by letter: Hibis Academy, Vretenv√§gen 13, 171 54 Solna, Sweden. In the event of cancellation, no payment will be required if Hibis Academy has been notified at least 2 weeks ahead of the course. 50% of the full price will be charged if Hibis Academy has been notified at least 1 week ahead of the course, and full price will be charged otherwise. If a registered course participant is unable to attend the course, his/her spot can be transferred to someone else, free of charge.

Payment terms

Payment on invoice 15 days, no later than the start of the course. All prices exclude VAT.

Personal data

Personal data will be stored and used by Hibis Academy for administration and fulfilment of the course, in accordance with laws pertaining to personal data in the country where the course is held. The data will not be shared with third parties. Personal data may be used by Hibis Academy for providing information about future courses.

Course Certificate

In order to receive a course certificate, participants must attend each day of the course. An attendance list will be held by the course leaders.

Accommodation

Hotel room reservations are the responsibility of the course participant.

About Hibis Academy

Hibis Academy is the education and training division of Hibis AB, registered in Sweden and Hibis AS, registered in Norway.
